TIC TAC FLOW
Only 3 pieces each · Shift to win
Each player has only 3 pieces: X1·X2·X3 and O1·O2·O3.

Placement (6 turns): X1, O1, X2, O2, X3 — then X1 gets a ● dot. O3 — then O1 gets a dot.

Movement: On your turn, the ● dotted piece must move to any empty cell. Its old spot goes vacant. Then the next piece in your rotation gets the dot.

Make a line of 3 to win — same as Tic Tac Toe.
